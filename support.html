Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new bike?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Enter your bike's make, model, and year to get started. You can add specs, a photo, and service history afterward.

How do service reminders work?

Riderr tracks your mileage and time between services. Once you set a reminder interval, you'll receive a notification when service is due.

What is Riderr Premium?

Premium unlocks unlimited bikes, iCloud sync across your devices, AI-powered invoice import, and the modification tracker. Available as a monthly subscription or one-time lifetime purchase.

How does the manual upload feature work?

You can upload a PDF of your owner's manual and Riderr will extract key specs like oil type, tire sizes, and service intervals automatically.

What is the Claude AI feature?

Riderr can use the Claude AI API to intelligently parse service invoices and import records. This requires a free API key from Anthropic, which you enter in Settings. Your key is stored securely on your device and sent only to Anthropic's servers.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Riderr Premium → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is my data backed up?

Premium subscribers get iCloud sync, which keeps your data backed up and synced across all your Apple devices automatically.
