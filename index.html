Features

Everything your bike needs.

Built for the rider who takes pride in knowing their machine inside and out.

Service History

Log every oil change, tire rotation, chain service, and more. Your full maintenance history in one place.

Smart Reminders

Get notified when service is due — by mileage, time interval, or season. Never let maintenance slip again.

Manual Upload

Upload your owner's manual as a PDF and Riderr automatically extracts oil type, tire sizes, chain specs, and service intervals.

Recall Alerts

Automatically checks NHTSA's database for open safety recalls on your bike's make, model, and year.

Mods Tracker

Keep a detailed log of every aftermarket part and upgrade. Know exactly what's on your build at all times.

iCloud Sync

Premium subscribers get seamless iCloud backup and sync across all their Apple devices. Your data always with you.

Never miss a service

Reminders that actually work.

Set service intervals by mileage or time. Riderr tracks your odometer and pings you when it's time — whether that's an oil change every 3,000 miles or a valve check every spring.

  • Mileage-based and time-based reminders
  • Seasonal prep reminders (winterize, de-winterize)
  • Customizable intervals for every service type

AI-powered specs

Upload your manual.
Get instant specs.

Drop in a PDF of your owner's manual and Riderr reads it for you. Oil viscosity, tire pressures, chain slack, torque specs — all extracted and saved to your bike's profile automatically.

  • Supports any PDF owner's manual
  • Powered by Claude AI (your own API key)
  • Editable after import — you're always in control
